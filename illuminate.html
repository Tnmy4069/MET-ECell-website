Illuminate Workshop 2023

Empowering Entrepreneurial Dreams

About Illuminate 2023

Illuminate 2023 is part of the prelude to Eureka!, Asia's largest Business Model Competition, organized by ECell, IIT Bombay. Aspiring entrepreneurs will have the opportunity to delve into the world of startups through engaging sessions with successful entrepreneurs, angel investors, IIT/IIM alumni, and seasoned mentors. This workshop series is open to students from diverse academic backgrounds, encouraging a mix of ideas and perspectives.

Why Illuminate?

  • Provide a platform for entrepreneurial learning.
  • Connect students with industry experts and successful entrepreneurs.
  • Equip participants with practical knowledge to kickstart, promote, and scale their ventures.
  • Foster an entrepreneurial mindset regardless of academic background.

Date, Time, and Venue

  • Date: 25th November 2023
  • Time: 10:00 AM to 5:30 PM
  • Venue: IOTP Seminar Hall, IOT BTech Building, MET Bhujbal Knowledge City, Adgaon, Nashik

Previous Year Highlights

  4. Session 4: Finance fundae and Marketing

  7. Session 7: The Art of Pitching
  8. Session 8: Final Pitch
